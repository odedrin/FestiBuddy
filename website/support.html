Support

Need help with DoseAngel, found a bug, or have an idea for a feature? Here's how to reach us.

Email support

We typically reply within a few days.

odedr@doseangel.com

Reporting a bug

To help us fix it quickly, please include:

  • Your device model and iOS version
  • The DoseAngel app version (Settings > About)
  • What you were doing right before the issue happened
  • A screenshot, if possible

Feature requests

We'd love to hear your ideas. Send them to the email above, and check the Roadmap section on the homepage to see what's already planned.

Common questions

Many common questions, like what substances are supported, how accurate the timing curves are, and whether DoseAngel stores your data, are answered on the FAQ section of the homepage.
