Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026

DoseAngel is built to work fully offline. This page explains what data the app collects (none) and what data this website collects (only if you choose to sign up for updates).

The DoseAngel app

DoseAngel does not require an account and does not connect to the internet to function. Everything you enter, including substances, timers, and plans, is stored locally on your device only.

  • No account or sign-in is required.
  • No data you enter is transmitted to us or to any third party.
  • The app includes no analytics, no advertising, and no crash-reporting SDKs.
  • Custom substance profiles you create stay on your device and are never uploaded anywhere.

You can review this in the app itself under Settings > Privacy & disclosure.

In short: if you delete the app, all of your data goes with it. We never see it, store it, or have any way to recover it.

This website (doseangel.com)

If you submit your email address using the "Get notified when DoseAngel launches" form, we store that address solely to send you launch updates and occasional news about DoseAngel. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email we send, or by contacting us directly.

Our hosting provider may temporarily log standard technical information (such as IP address and browser type) for security and performance purposes. This is not used to identify or track individual visitors.

Substance and interaction reference data

The timing and interaction reference data built into the app is sourced from publicly published information from PsychonautWiki and TripSit. This is static reference content shipped with the app; it is not personalized and does not involve any of your data being sent anywhere.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as DoseAngel evolves. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date at the top.

Contact
